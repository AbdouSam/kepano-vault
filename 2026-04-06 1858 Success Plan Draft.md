This is what i have learned from [[Dave van Vliet]] on schedule setting. Key points

- You must have a yearly plan you work towards
- You must have a five year vision that you approach to it
- You must decompose your yearly goal into monthly Goals and review them monthly
- you must set Weekly Goals inline with the Monthly and the yearly
- You must set daily goals

### Auto-suggestion and Faith

the mind is plastic, the mind believes what ever is primed to do. Prime your mind daily. Your day starts when you open your eyes in the morning. Up until that moment the mind was in a **no-mind** state where everything is possible, that is why in dreams you can fly but also if feels real when you are hurt.

At the wake-up moment, the mind tries to **wear the personality** it knows, which is you, you memories in your brain and your habits learned and stored by your body. Also everything around you identifies who you are and reminds you constantly of your identity. the mirror reminds you how you look and all the triggers that are related to it. your thoughts remind you how you feel towards the thing you see. etc....

**Waking up in the morning, the first thing to do before your mind takes over is to prime your mind by auto-suggesting positive idea.**
