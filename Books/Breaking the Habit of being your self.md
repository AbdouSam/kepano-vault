The Core premise of the book is **To Change is to think greater than the three restrictions: your environment, your body and time

### Practical steps

#### 1. Un-memorize  the old

while setting before engaging into the daily life, walk through your day with your mind while sitting in a comfortable un-interrupted and start noticing the things you wish you change. create triggers.

One of the most destructive old-habits is the habit of **Poor thinking** or **Negative Thinking**

The idea is **to become conscious of your unconscious self, by observing without judgement**

Along with all the bad habits, once you become conscious you will have a notification in your mind when the negative thought happens, at this moment you break the link, but saying "Change" and you re-direct.

#### 2. Build a new self

the practice is summarized in :

1. Mental inventory: Become familiar with the old self, know it, list the habits and emotions and behaviour you want to address or change.

2. Enter the No mind:  [[Daily meditation]] the place to change the habit cannot be the self itself, because wiring is already in place. you must enter the no-mind state to see the habits from a distance

3. Visualization with Elevated emotion: see your self being that person, walking, talking acting like it. **elevated emotion is necessary, if you don't have emotions with the visualization, nothing will happen**

4. Daily repetition #persistence nothing can replace the daily hammering for any idea or skill

5. Intention setting. we talked about this a different location, Set the intention for the day and the night before sleeping.
