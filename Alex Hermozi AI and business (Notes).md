stop thinking on role based thinking, and start thinking on workflow base thinking

So the idea is to think about what business needs in terms of functions and outputs and erase the titles that exists.

  
Advice from Alex Ramosi.
He said that is what he can do.  
- Write everything you do in a single day to the granular level.
- Separate all you do into tasks. For example, sending email, working on marketing, etc.
- Be very detailed. Do not just say, for example, "I create video." What do you mean? Do you shoot the video? Do you edit the video? Et cetera. Be detailed, as detailed as possible.
- Go through the list one by one and take the first one. Ask AI to help you automate the task.
